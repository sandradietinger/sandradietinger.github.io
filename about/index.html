About me

Some insights and facts

Hi, I am Sandra Kragl

a UX researcher / designer committed to make people’s life easier.

My motto is, "Solving the right problem for the right user!". I am eager to build intuitive applications to make people’s life easier.

I grew up in the beautiful countryside of Upper Austria. Now I live in the culture-rich and vibrant city of Vienna.
I studied Media Informatics at the Technical University of Vienna (Bachelor and Master) and specialized in the area of Human Computer Interaction. Furthermore, I am a trained kindergarten teacher. This knowledge has frequently turned out to be essential for conscious interaction with humans and the investigation of their behavior.

I am currently working as a UX researcher/designer at kununu. There my days are filled with the desire to improve the knowledge about users and customers in order to create an experience that empowers people to find the right employer/employee. If you ask my colleagues, they would describe me as a team player who is passionate, motivated and fun to work with.

In my free time, I like to travel around the world or sew anything that comes to mind. My last big sewing project was a Jedi costume. One of my favourite travelling adventures was exploring New Zealand in a campervan - it was amazing!
