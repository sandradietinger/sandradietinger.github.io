Hi, I am Sandra Dietinger.

My motto is, "Solving the right problem for the right user!". I am very eager to build intuitive applications, which easy the life of people.
I grew up in the beautiful countryside of Upper Austria. Now I live in the culture-rich and vibrant city of Vienna.
I'm currently working as a Product/UX Designer at Automic Software. If you ask my colleagues, they would describe me as very passionate, motivated, a good team player and fun to work with.
I studied media informatics at the Technical University of Vienna (bachelor and master). Furthermore, I am a trained kindergarten teacher. This knowledge has repeatedly turned out to be essential for conscious contact with humans and the investigation of their behaviour.
In my leisure time, I like to travel around the world or sew what ever comes to my mind (my last big project was a Jedi costume).
